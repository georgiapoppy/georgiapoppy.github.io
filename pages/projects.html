WORDLE

Implementation of the New York Times game 'Wordle'. A random 5 letter word is chosen, and you have 6 trys to guess it. For each guess, you're awarded with green squares if the letter is in the right spot, yellow if the letter is in the word but in the wrong spot, and grey if it's not in the word at all.

This project was coded with the PyGame library in Python!
